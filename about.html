About HerCycleCalc

HerCycleCalc makes the maths of fertility, pregnancy and the early years simple, with gentle, judgement-free tools you can use privately, for free.

What we do

We build clear, fast calculators for the questions that come up when you're trying to conceive, expecting, or finding your feet with a new baby: when am I most fertile, when is the baby due, how far along am I, what's happening this week, and what should I be sorting out next. Everything runs in your browser, there's nothing to sign up for and we don't store your dates.

Editorial standards

Our calculators use well-established methods (such as the 280-day count from your last period) and our guidance reflects typical UK NHS antenatal pathways. We aim to be accurate, plain-spoken and kind. Where a topic is sensitive or clinical, we point you to trusted sources like the NHS and Tommy's rather than offering opinions of our own.

This is not medical advice. Our results are estimates. Every pregnancy and cycle is different, and only a healthcare professional who knows your situation can give you personal advice. Always speak to your GP, midwife or health visitor about anything that matters to you.

Privacy

We designed HerCycleCalc to be private. Your dates and inputs are processed entirely on your device. Anything you "save" is stored in your own browser's local storage, it never reaches our servers, and clearing your browser data removes it. We don't ask you to create an account, and we don't track your individual figures.
