Trigger Shot Test-Out Calculator

An hCG trigger shot (like Ovitrelle, Pregnyl or Novarel) can show up on a home pregnancy test for days afterwards. "Testing out" means testing daily until the trigger fades to negative, so a later positive is more likely to be real. This estimates when your trigger should clear.
